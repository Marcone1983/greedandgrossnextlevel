4. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I upgrade to Premium?
A: Go to Settings > Subscription > Manage Plan. Choose your preferred billing cycle (monthly or annual) and complete the purchase through your app store.
Q: Can I use this app if cannabis is illegal in my area?
A: GREED & GROSS is an educational simulation. However, you should check your local laws regarding cannabis-related content. The app is intended for educational purposes only.
Q: How accurate are the breeding simulations?
A: Our simulations are based on simplified genetic models for educational purposes. They provide a general understanding of inheritance patterns but are not scientifically precise.
Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes! Premium subscribers can export their strain library, breeding history, and progress data through Settings > Data Export.
Q: Is my data backed up?
A: Yes, all user data is automatically backed up to secure cloud storage. Premium users get additional backup features and cross-device sync.
Q: How do I report inappropriate content?
A: Use the report button in chat or contact support@greedandgross.com. We take community standards seriously and investigate all reports.
Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime through your app store subscription settings. You'll continue to have Premium access until the end of your billing period.

5. Troubleshooting

5.1 Common Issues

App Won't Start:

  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates
  • Clear app cache (Android)
  • Reinstall the app if necessary

Login Problems:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your email and password
  • Try "Forgot Password" if needed
  • Contact support if using Google Sign-In

Chat Not Working:

  • Check your network connection
  • Restart the app
  • Check if you've reached daily message limits (Free tier)
  • Verify you're not blocked or suspended

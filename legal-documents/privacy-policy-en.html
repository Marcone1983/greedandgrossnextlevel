Privacy Policy

Language: English
Last Updated: December 19, 2024
Effective Date: December 19, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to GREED & GROSS, a cannabis breeding simulation application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Important: GREED & GROSS is designed for educational and entertainment purposes only. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and profile picture (if provided)
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • User-Generated Content: Messages, strain creations, and simulation data

2.2 Usage Information

  • App Usage: Features used, time spent, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics for app improvement

2.3 Technical Information

  • Log Data: Error logs, crash reports, and performance metrics
  • Network Information: IP address and connection status

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Provide and maintain the GREED & GROSS simulation experience
  • Process your account registration and authentication
  • Enable chat functionality and social features
  • Save your progress and simulation data
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

3.2 Secondary Uses

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Develop new features and content
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activity

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (Firebase, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Public Content: Chat messages and user-generated content visible to other users

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services provided by Google Cloud Platform, with servers located in the United States and other regions.

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Account Access: View and update your profile information
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data (Premium feature)
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Communication Preferences: Control notification settings

6.2 Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Chat messages are retained for 30 days
  • Analytics data is anonymized after 90 days
  • Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days

7. Third-Party Services

GREED & GROSS integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and analytics
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and billing
  • Expo: Push notifications and updates

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

8. Children's Privacy

GREED & GROSS is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use this app or provide any personal information.

9. International Users

If you are accessing GREED & GROSS from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Legal Disclaimer

Educational Purpose Only: GREED & GROSS is a simulation app for educational and entertainment purposes. We do not encourage, promote, or provide guidance for actual cannabis cultivation. Users are responsible for complying with all applicable local, state, and federal laws.
